The Great Panda (one of the main Panda species) is considered an endangered species with only 1000 of them left in China. In general, Pandas live in temperate forests high in the mountains of China, and one of their only food sources is bamboo. female Pandas can grow up to about 200 pounds, while male Pandas can grow up to about 300 pounds as adults.
